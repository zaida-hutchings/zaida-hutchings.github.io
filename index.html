Released Video Games

This section are all completed projects or upcoming projects Zaida has worked on. They have all been uploaded to itch.io. This was last updated January 2026.

Leave A Message

If the world was ending, what would your last words be? Who would you say it to? Leave a Message is a 3d narrative game where you play as the world's last messenger. This was created for the UGA Game Builder's Club for the Fall of 2024 as a semester-long project. I was the team lead for the project. I pitched the game, managed ten people, designed and wrote the storyline, and made a theme song.

Mirror Mirror: Erebus's Layer

Trapped inside a nightmarish land, play solo or with up to four friends to stealthly escape the Ink Monster before it consumes you. Mirror Mirror is a year-long on going project led by Zaida Hutchings. She pitched the game, and is managing her team (currently with nine members), doing the 3d Art and animation for the player characters, and fully designing the game. It is planned to be released at the end of summer of 2026. This is an independent project, but it was made alongside the Game Builder's CLub at UGA.

T-minus 5 Minutes

Arriving on a space station 5 minutes before it collapse, Stella Starbreak must find a way to survive. Explore a station of 10 rooms with 6 distinct ending. Will you escape the station, save your new friends or fall to your doom. This was created for the UGA Game Builder's Club for the Spring of 2025 as a semester-long project. I was primarily an artist for the game, and I programed one of the mini-games. I did the NPC sprites and animations. I also helped with environmental art.

Making A List: And Checking it Twice

In Making a List: Checking it Twice, it has been a bad day at work for Santa. Nutcrackers and Gingerbread men are rioting and have taken the elves hostage! Time to get down and dirty, and let them know who's been naughty and who's been nice. This was a 48-hour game jam with several universities across Georgia. Making A List ranked 2nd best overall. I worked with three other students. I helped with music, enemy design and animation, and environmental and UI art.
